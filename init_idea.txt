FIFA Career Narrative Companion is an offline desktop sidecar for FIFA/FC career mode. You play the game yourself;
the app does not read or modify saves.

It helps you run a career more deliberately by giving you:

- Club context — philosophy, budget limits, and era-specific narrative per year or years for your team.
- Objectives & matches — board goals and important fixtures to track manually (based on rivalry and distance in km).
- Roster tools — search players and get signing suggestions that fit your club and budget.
  Ex-players of the team or future players of the team if I play Fifa 15 and that player signs for a team in Fifa 18
  must enter in signing suggests.
- Journal — notes per season.
- Club archive — browse how clubs changed across FIFA/FC editions.

Dataset covers from FIFA 15 to FIFA 20.

In the first section you should select FIFA version and team of your Career Mode. You have Club Archive in this first
section too. Then you enter in other options menu: Club Context, Objectives & Matches, Roster Tools, Journal.
